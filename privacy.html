📋 About This Policy

LetsGo is a ride booking app developed by Youssef Elbedwehy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: February 2025

📦 Data We Collect

  • Name and Phone Number — for account creation and ride booking
  • Location — to match you with nearby drivers and track your trip
  • Ride History — to show your past trips inside the app

💳 Payments

All payments are processed securely through Tap Payment Gateway. LetsGo does not store, access, or process any payment or card details. All financial data is handled entirely by Tap Payments in accordance with their security standards.

🔗 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Your data is used solely to provide the ride booking service.

🔒 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Location data is only accessed while using the app to provide the service.

👶 Children's Privacy

LetsGo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
